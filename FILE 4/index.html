Photography

Product Advertisement

A client want us to photograph their product in a creative and visually captivating way. The photographs will be used across different social media platforms and be used on the client’s website, in selected quality magazines, posters and billboards. Therefore, it is vital that the composition of the photograph allows for landscape, portrait and square orientations. In this task we are asked to shoot a high-quality advertising photograph of a product of our choice. I choose to create a photos for a skin care product called the “Creightons Pink Grapefruit Refreshing Face Wash”. It is a face wash product that is made specifically for women that want to look and feel good. It is applicable to all skin types and it is vegan friendly.

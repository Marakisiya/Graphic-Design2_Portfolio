Semester Project

Public Awareness Campaign

The semester project involves the design of a logo and brand touchpoints for a public awareness campaign of our choice. The primary purpose of a public awareness campaign is to address groups of people to be aware of an issue and hopefully change their behavior. In this task we are asked to identify a bad or unwanted habit, investigate if certain environment contributed to the habit and if so, we should devise some way using graphic design to disrupt that environment. For the project I choose to work on creating public awareness about Female Genital Mutilation (FGM) practice. This is problem that is affecting many young girls in many parts of the world. According to UNICEF, around 4 million girls (under the age of 15) worldwide are at risk of undergoing FGM each year. FGM is a violation of girls’ and women’s fundamental human rights.

Strategic Design

Brodies Books - Rare Books & Coffe Brews

In this assignment we were tasked with the design of a branding system for the business. The new branding system should convey the atmosphere and feeling of the business in a better way. We are asked to ensure the following are included in the design: New brand identity including a logo, typography, colour scheme and overall visual style. The logo must contain the name “Brodie’s Books”, and an additional version of the logo with the words “Rare Books & Coffee Brews”. Vehicle branding: it must contain the logo, website (brodiesbooks.co.uk) and physical address (21 High Street, Edinburgh), and the logo should be applied to the bicycle as well. The version of the logo with the byline “Rare Books & Coffee Brews” should be used on the van. A new packaging for the coffee called Brodie’s Brew. This should be a conceptual design that shows a unique and strategic approach to coffee packaging that will tie in with the rest of their brand and the products where to be delivered together with a Presentation and a brand style manual.
